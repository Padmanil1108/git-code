
ls – Lists all files and folders in your current directory.
cd – Changes your working directory to a specified path.
pwd – Prints the absolute path of your current directory.
mkdir – Creates a new folder or directory
.rmdir – Removes an empty directory.
rm – Deletes files permanently; use rm -r for directories.
touch – Creates a blank file or updates timestamps.
cp – Copies files or folders to a new location
.mv – Moves or renames files and directories.
