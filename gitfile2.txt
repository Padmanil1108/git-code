System Monitoring and Process Control


top / htop – Provides a dynamic, live view of active processes.
ps aux – Snapshots all processes running on the system.
kill – Terminates a process using its Process ID (PID).
df -h – Displays available and used disk space cleanly.
free -m – Displays total, used, and free RAM allocations.
systemctl – Manages background system service states (start, stop, restart)
